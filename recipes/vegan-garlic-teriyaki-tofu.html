Vegan Garlic Teriyaki Tofu

This Garlic Teriyaki Tofu recipe is perfect if you are a beginner vegan, and itâ€™s also high in protein and a flavor-bomb!

Ingredients

  • extra firm tofu (6.5 oz/people or serving)
  • soy sauce
  • teriyaki glaze, but a thick teriyaki sauce is good as well
  • garlic
  • oil
  • sesame seeds

Steps

  1. Cut up the tofu into small cubes.
  2. Heat the oil in a pan on medium heat. When the oil is hot, add the tofu cubes and start frying them on all sides. Your tofu cubes are ready when all sides are golden colored. When your tofu cubes are fried, add the soy sauce and fry everything evenly for 1/2-1 minute. This step is very important to make the tofu crispy.
  3. Turn off the heat, then add the teriyaki glaze and minced garlic. Stir everything together in the pan for 1 minute.
  4. Add the sesame seeds.
  5. Serve over fresh rice and add your optional garnish.
